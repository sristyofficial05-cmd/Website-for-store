About Our Shop

PATWA JI is a traditional Mithila wedding and puja store located in Madhubani, Bihar. We specialize in Mithila Paag, wedding garlands (maala), and all types of marriage ritual items.

Our shop is dedicated to preserving the rich cultural heritage of Mithila by providing authentic and high-quality handcrafted products for weddings, puja, and religious ceremonies.

We have been serving customers with trust, quality, and tradition. Our goal is to bring Mithila culture to every home.
